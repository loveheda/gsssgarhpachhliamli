Welcome to G.S.S.S. garhpachhliamli

Discover Why
Our Student Choose Us

Established in 1960, Government Senior Secondary School (G.S.S.S.) garh pachhli amli has been a cornerstone of education in the Suwana block of Bhilwara district, Rajasthan. Managed by the Department of Education, our school provides quality education from Grade 1 to Grade 12 in a co-educational environment, with Hindi as the medium of instruction.

Principal

Ratan Lal Bhat

At G.S.S.S. garh pachhli amli, we believe in empowering students with knowledge, values, and skills to shape a brighter future. Our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds in a supportive and inclusive environment.

  • Well-Equipped Classrooms

    8 spacious and well-maintained classrooms for a conducive learning environment.

  • Library & Digital Learning

    A vast collection of 10,258 books and 10 functional computers for interactive learning.

  • Experienced & Dedicated Faculty

    Committed to academic excellence and holistic development.

  • Safe & Accessible Campus

    Easily reachable by all-weather roads, with a playground and essential amenities.

OUR STORY

From Where We
Started Our Journey

  • 1960

    Establishment

    Govt. Sr. Sec. School garhpachhliamli was established, initially offering only a Primary section.

  • 1972

    Upgraded to Upper Primary

    Recognizing the need for higher education, the school was promoted to Upper Primary level.

  • 1989

    Promoted to Secondary Level

    After 14 years of growth and academic excellence, the school was elevated to the Secondary level.

  • 2015

    Upgraded to Senior Secondary Level

    Owing to its outstanding academic and sports achievements, the school received promotion to Senior Secondary level.

  • Present Day

    A Thriving Educational Hub

    • Consistently delivering top academic and sports performances.
    • Introducing Vocational Training in IT/ITes trade.
    • Over 380 students enrolled from Class 1 to 12.
    • Offering Arts stream.
