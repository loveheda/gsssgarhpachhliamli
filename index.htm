About Us

Our Story: Built On Values, Driven By Inspiration

Govt Sr Sec School garhpachhliamli was founded in 1941. At present more than 660 students study here from class 1-12 and the school has the honour of being the Adarsh Vidhyalaya of phase 1 under the flagship scheme of Rajasthan Government. All the streams i.e. Science, Arts, Commerce are available along with the vocational trade (IT/ITes and Automobile) for students.

The school takes pride inthe image of being an institution which always gives best results in terms of education, along with character building and providing good citizens to the society.

  • Our Mission:

    Our goal is to provide good education that helps students think, learn, and grow. We want to make sure every student gains knowledge, skills, and strong values to succeed in life.

  • Our Vision

    We dream of making our school a place of great learning, where students become responsible, skilled, and confident. We want to prepare them for the future by giving them the right education and values to do well in life and help society.
